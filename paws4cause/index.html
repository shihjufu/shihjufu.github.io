About

Each month, 400 volunteers come to help the people who work at the animal shelter and the dogs and cats. The shelter only has 200 cages for the abused and abandoned dogs and cats of Suzhou. Eighty percent of the animals are injured, and half of them are puppies. It only takes one week to adopt a pet from the Animal Shelter. In this shelter, about 40 dogs get adopted each month, but 10 new dogs come every week to replace the dogs that are already happily adopted, which makes no change at all to the problems in the shelter. The dogs and cats there need your help!
